WishRoll

March 2020 - Present

Cofounder, Android Developer

WishRoll is a reaction picture, video, and gif sesarch engine and social media website. Much like Giphy, users can search for any reaction media they want. I noticed teens did not use gifs from the likes of Giphy and Tenor because they weren't funny or expressive enough. With WishRoll, people are now able to use short-form video clips and current reaction pictures to express how they feel in any messaging app.


Cofounded WishRoll along with Great Okonkwo. Contributed as an Android Developer, UX Designer, Marketing Director, and Product Manager. Curated all social media presences and put out a social media campaign that got over 1.8 million views on TikTok and grew brand page to 10k followers in a week. Designed the WishRoll Logo, official website, and various app layouts. Put together a social media brand ambassador campaign and planned several social media advertisements. Worked on Android Client
